The much-anticipated trailer of Nisho, Mehazabien starrer “Neel Joler Kabbyo” is set to release on Thursday. The Shihab Shaheen directorial web-film is scheduled to be released on the popular OTT platform iScreen on November 16.<\/p>\n

Poster of the film<\/h3>\n

After the immense success of the web-film “Redrum”, the Afran Nisho and Mehazabien Chowdhury duo were not seen in any OTT content together. They will however come together for this web-film by Shihab Shaheen. The platform had already revealed the first poster of the film on November 1, on their social media. The poster shows Mehazabien and Nisho standing by the seashore, holding hands, during the twilight hours. Confirming the release, director Shihab Shaheen said, “The audience will get to see a fantastic story in this film. Besides, Nisho and Mehazabien have a dedicated fan base. I believe that even the general audience will enjoy the content.”<\/p>\n
